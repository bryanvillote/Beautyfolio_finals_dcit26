An ultra-fine setting powder that blurs imperfections and visibly reduces shine.

Usage:
-Using the Sculpt Brush (sold separately), apply Airbrush Flawless Finish around the nose, T-zone, and any other oily areas that need to be mattified.
-It can be used on its own, or on top of foundation and concealer to set them in place. ­­
-For oily skin types looking for a truly flawless finish, apply Airbrush Flawless Finish all over the face for more coverage, starting from the T-zone and working outwards and upwards.

Medium Coverage
