This dual-ended blush stick offers sheer, buildable coverage with a dewy finish in a moisturizing formula ideal for all skin types.

Usage:
-Smile softly to find the apples of your cheeks and apply the blush stick to the higher part of the apples towards the hairline.
-Gently blend outward toward the hairline with the built-in brush to lift.
-Five nubs within the head of the brush centralize product pick-up for a more even, fluid application.
-Thoughtfully-designed angled buffing brush makes application easy and is removable for convenient cleaning.

Recommendations:
Fits a Neutral undertone
Can be used in all Complexions
Best for all skin types:
Combo, Dry, Oily and Sensitive
