A lightweight, refillable, pressed finishing powder that layers smoothly over foundation for a fresh, soft-matte finish that lasts all day.

Usage:
-For all-over application, use the Lock-It Precision Powder Brush (sold separately) to dust on and mattify skin.
-Pat and roll Lock-It Finishing Powder using the cushion-puff applicator anywhere you need extra mattifying.

Refill Product Instructions:
-To refill, insert a pin into the hole on the back of the compact.

Recommendations:
Can be used in all Complexions
Best for all skin types:
Combination, Dry, Oily and Sensitive
