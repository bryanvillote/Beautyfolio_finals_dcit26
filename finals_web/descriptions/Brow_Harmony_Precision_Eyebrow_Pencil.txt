A waterproof, sweat- and oil-resistant pencil with a fine-angled tip to create precise, hair-like strokes for the most natural-looking brows.

Usage:
-Twist pencil to reveal a small amount of product.
-Use the precision angled tip to fill in brows by drawing short, hair-like strokes in the direction of your natural hair growth.
-Once brows are filled in, blend with spoolie brush to achieve your desired look.

Recommendations:
Can be used in all Complexions
Best for all skin types:
Combination, Dry, Oily and Sensitive
